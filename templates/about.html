About Bernoulli

What is Bernoulli?

Bernoulli is a system for transforming natural speech into mathematical expressions. It gives users the opportunity to express math in the most natural medium available - the human voice.

Why use Bernoulli?

It is often difficult and time-consuming to write LaTeX or other forms of mathematics equations on the computer. Bernoulli is an easy, simple way to mitigate this problem by auto-generating LaTeX based on voice input.

How was Bernoulli created?

Bernoulli was developed in 7 hours at CodeDay Detroit Winter 2015 by several high school students. The development stack includes Python, Flask, jQuery, Google Web Speech API, and Bootstrap. The source code is available here.
